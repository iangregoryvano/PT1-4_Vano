The music that I like are actually quite broad,
I can say that I like almost all music genres.
I appreciate music because it has a rich history,
and relieves stress for me when things get rough.
I think I also have some talent for music,
and I think that's why I resonate with it so much.
I appreciate music that expresses deep emotions and messages.
When I do become an artist in the future,
I will aim to express experiences and messages as well,
as a fragment of my creative power.
